Account privacy

Private account
Only approved followers can see your photos and videos.

When your account is public, your profile and posts can be seen by anyone on or off InsChat, even if they don't have an account.

When your account is private, only followers you approve can see what you share, including your photos or videos on hashtags and location pages, and your followers and following lists.

Certain information on your profile, like your profile photo and username, will still be visible to everyone.
